Directions

The Institute is on the upper east side of Manhattan, just a block from Central Park and the Metropolitan Museum of Art. There are many parking garages near the Institute and the Subway is close by.

Subway: The nearest Subway stop is at 86th St. and Lexington Ave. It is served by the ➍, ➎ and ➏ trains. The Institute is a short walk from this station. For detailed directions, please use the MTA Trip Planner.
